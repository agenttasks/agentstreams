    Plugin Directory Structure (standard):
    ──────────────────────────────────────
    plugin-name/
    ├── .claude-plugin/
    │   └── plugin.json             # {"name","description","version"}
    ├── .mcp.json                   # {"mcpServers": {"name": {"type","url"}}}
    ├── skills/
    │   └── skill-slug/
    │       └── SKILL.md            # ---\nname: ...\ndescription: ...\n---\n# Body
    ├── commands/                   # Slash commands (explicit triggers)
    ├── CONNECTORS.md               # Connector documentation
    └── README.md
